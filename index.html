SHAP — TreeExplainer
Exact Shapley values for XGBoost model. Sparse TF-IDF input is converted to dense arrays (sample of 200 test instances). Produces summary plots and per-instance waterfall charts.
LIME — LimeTextExplainer
Local surrogate model fitted around a perturbed neighborhood of a single instance. 10 most influential features highlighted per prediction — class-aware for both SMS (Ham/Spam) and Email (Safe/Phishing).
